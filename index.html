🚫 rules we don't break

12:45 AM means 12:45 AM. the Bandipur forest gate is shut 9 PM–6 AM. leave on time → we cross at 6 sharp → Ooty by breakfast. leave late → we sit at a gate watching trucks.
no ghat driving after dark. monsoon + hairpins + night is the one genuinely dangerous combo on this trip. everything else is vibes.
check Google Maps every morning. monsoon closes roads randomly. 2 min of checking saves 2 hrs of rerouting.
no swimming. anywhere. the sea is angry and the waterfalls are angrier. we look, we click pics, we live.
